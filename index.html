ML/AI for Bio-EIS Signal Analysis for Cultured Meat Monitoring

Nanyang Technological University Aug 2025 – Present

Supervisor: Assoc. Prof. Poenar Daniel Puiu

Leading the development of a machine learning pipeline for bioelectrical impedance analysis on a microfluidic biochip platform, targeting automated classification and concentration estimation of microbead materials for cultured meat monitoring applications.

  • Designing and executing a structured EIS data acquisition protocol across multiple microbead materials at 3 concentration levels, building a statistically robust dataset for downstream ML model training
  • Performing Bode and Nyquist plot analysis to extract physically interpretable features; constructing Equivalent Circuit Models to validate alignment between theoretical physics constraints and real-world impedance measurements
  • Developing the ML pipeline targeting bead-type classification, concentration estimation, and mixture composition inference, with planned integration of physics-informed constraints via Maxwell-Hanai mixture theory
  • Conducting rigorous statistical reproducibility analysis across 6 biochips (18 measurements), evaluating intra-chip and inter-chip variability using coefficient of variation metrics
